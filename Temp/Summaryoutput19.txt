Turkey accuses Syria of harboring Turkish Kurdish rebels fighting for autonomy in Turkey s southeast it says rebel leader Abdullah Ocalan lives in Damascus
For its part Syria has accused Turkey of forming military alliances with Israel that threaten Arab security and undermine Syria s bargaining position in peace talks with the Jewish state
Damascus also accuses Turkey of building a series of dams on the Euphrates River that threaten to reduce Syria s water supply
Egypt already has launched a mediation effort to try to prevent a military confrontation over Turkey s allegations that Syria is harboring Turkish Kurdish rebels
Syria denied the allegation
